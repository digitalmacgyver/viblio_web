Search for your approximate location by clicking on the search icon (pic) and entering address, city, state, etc.

Zoom in and out of the map by clicking on the [+] and [-] (pics) buttons.

Drop a draggable marker (pic) by clicking on the map near the location you want.

Drag the (pic) markerover the exact location you want.

Click on the (pic) marker to cancel or choose this as your final location. If you cancel, you can drop another (pic) marker somewhere else.

Resize and drag the map around by moving your mouse to the bottom or right edges of the map and dragging.
