To manage an identified face, just click on it. Then you can:
  1. Update the person's name by clicking on it in the dark blue 'manage' panel
  2. Choose their default image by hovering over the photo you like in the manage panel and clicking the
  3. Remove images of the person by hovering over the image's name tag and clicking 'click to reject'
  4. Add images of the person by clicking the below their face in the 'Unknown People' panel
  5. Finish managing the face by clicking the 'Done' button in the manage panel
