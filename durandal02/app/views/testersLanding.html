JOIN the VIBLIO Beta Testers Club!

VIBLIO is a new private video platform that makes it easy to manage and share personal videos in private groups.

If you are a video taker interested in trying this platform and helping us test VIBLIO to make it as awesome, join the Testers Club. We’re looking for 50 testers.

In return for helping us test the platform and giving us your feedback, you'll get

  • an extra 10 GB of video storage space FREE!
  • one free automatically generated video mashup* for every video album you create

AND if you refer another tester who signs up for the platform, we'll also give you a VIBLIO beta-backer t-shirt. You will also be entered into a draw for a free MeCam wearable video camera.

To join, just enter your email below, and then create your new VIBLIO account.

  1. Start uploading, organizing and sharing your videos.
  2. Give us feedback through the feedback selection under your Account Settings or by sending an email to feedback@viblio.com
  3. Invite a friend using the “invite a friend” selection under your Account Settings to get a free VIBLIO t-shirt.

VIBLIO is private — no one can see your videos unless you specifically share a video with someone. VIBLIO is organized — you can easily filter through your videos by where they were taken or the faces in them! VIBLIO is community — videos are a great way to engage your friends and family and now it is easy to create community shared albums using VIBLIO.

* A VIBLIO video mashup is a mashup of video clips from all the videos in your album, stitched together in a fun short summary video.
