What is VIBLIO Photo Finder™

VIBLIO Photo Finder is an easy way to get even more out of your videos! Use our free Photo Finder to automatically get photos from the videos you have in your VIBLIO account.

  • Search for a set of videos in your VIBLIO account. (Remember you can search by title, date, place or face, or by tags you assigned, or by albums you created.)
  • When you have the videos you want, simply move the photo/video toggle from video to photo.

It’s as easy as that. For each video in your search results, we'll find at least one picture, and up to 30 pictures, depending on the length of the video.

If your search pulled up a lot of long videos, you can imagine getting pages of photo results, so we help you simplify by letting you tell us how many photo you want us to create, using this simple 3-point sliding scale.

How are the best photo shots chosen?

This is where our image processing technology kicks in. First, we find a clear face in a photo because we usually love photos of the people we film! Our technology knows how to look for clear faces. VIBLIO also looks for other elements that that will make for a good photo.

Our photo search is fully automated. (We don’t have people or tiny elves looking through your private videos!) So we might not get it perfect. If we find a photo you don’t love, it’s easy to delete it from your photo album. Just choose the option to delete under the More tab.

What can I do with my VIBLIO created photos?

If you like a photo that we have found for you, you can download it. Just click or tap the download icon in the top left corner of the photo.

Here's another fun thing you can do with your VIBLIO images. Select the ones that you want and create a Facebook photo album with a single click. Look for that feature under the Create tab when you are in Photo mode.

Who can see my VIBLIO created photos?

No one sees your photos or your videos unless you decide to share them. Here’s how it works.

  1. If you share a video with someone, they'll also be able to see the photos VIBLIO finds in that video.
  2. Because we know that people like to share photos with their friends, we also let people that you have invited to view your video album also download the photos that you have created from that video album. But we never let them download the entire video.
  3. If you create a Facebook photo album through VIBLIO, that album will get shared on your Facebook timeline according to your Facebook privacy settings.
